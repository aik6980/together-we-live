Love Gunner & Hat Runner

(a tale of Love and Hat for 2 players)

Instructions: Unite the ghosts with Love Gunner and Hat Runner.
Love Gunner can stun the mean & scary ghosts.
Hat Runner can collect the stunned ghosts and return them to Love Gunner!
Fill your Love% to Win!
